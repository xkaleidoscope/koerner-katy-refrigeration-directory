Commercial ice machine repair questions for Katy operators

Ice machine failures can hit restaurants, bars, hotels, and convenience stores fast. The right questions reduce guesswork before a service call.

Capture the issue

  • Low production, no production, leaking, noise, or cloudy ice.
  • Water filter age and cleaning schedule.
  • Brand and model, such as Hoshizaki, Manitowoc, Scotsman, or Ice-O-Matic.
  • Any recent power, drain, or water-supply change.

Compare service quality

  • Does the provider service the brand?
  • Can they handle cleaning and repair?
  • Do they document sanitation-related findings?
  • Do they offer maintenance options after the fix?
