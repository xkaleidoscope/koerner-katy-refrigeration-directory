Walk-in cooler repair questions for Katy food businesses

A cooler that will not hold temperature can turn into lost product, failed prep, and rushed contractor decisions. Use this checklist before choosing a repair path.

No live dispatch No fake provider claim Katy / Fulshear focus

Document before the call

  • Current box temperature and target temperature.
  • When the drift started and whether doors were propped open.
  • Brand, model, age, and recent service history.
  • Visible ice, fan noise, water leaks, or gasket damage.

Ask the provider

  • Do you service this equipment brand?
  • Do you carry common parts on the truck?
  • What does after-hours response actually mean?
  • Is there a parts and labor warranty?
