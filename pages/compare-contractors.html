Open provider evaluation checklist

What the staged asset can prove now

  • It covers multiple commercial refrigeration intents instead of relying on one generic city page.
  • It can show equipment-specific and emergency-decision content patterns drawn from public provider pages.
  • It keeps the trust boundary visible by blocking lead capture, dispatch claims, and fake local-business framing.

What a publishable version would still need

  • Real provider ownership, approved contact routing, and truthful service-area proof.
  • Real license, insurance, review, photo, response-time, and warranty evidence if claimed.
  • Privacy terms, consent language, and provider-approved conversion rails before any intake exists.

Kill conditions for this lane

  • No fake office, fake "near me" locality, or copied provider identity.
  • No invented testimonials, rankings, leads, revenue, or conversion guarantees.
  • No publish, form, phone, email, SMS, or dispatch action until explicit authority exists.
