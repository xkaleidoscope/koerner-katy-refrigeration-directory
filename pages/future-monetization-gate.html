What would make this commercially reviewable

Asset proof

  • Static directory pages render cleanly and are internally linked.
  • Every category has a useful operator decision guide.
  • No provider quality claim appears without evidence.

Provider proof

  • Real provider identity, scope, service area, warranty, and routing terms.
  • License, insurance, reviews, and photos only if supplied and approved.
  • No fake local presence, copied reviews, or unowned credentials.

Rail proof

  • Approved privacy, consent, payment, disclosure, and routing terms.
  • Approved publish or no-publish decision.
  • Explicit authority before any buyer outreach, provider contact, or live testing.
